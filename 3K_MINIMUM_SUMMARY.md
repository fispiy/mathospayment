# Why 3K Minimum Model is Best - Summary

## Cost Savings
• **63.5% savings vs Current Model:** $11,625 reduction ($18,310 → $6,685)
• **51.2% savings vs CPM Model:** $7,008 reduction ($13,693 → $6,685)
• **Best cost per view:** $0.001725 (63% lower than Model 1's $0.004656)
• **Best CPM:** $1.73 per 1,000 views (beats industry standard $2-$5)

## Model Comparison
• **Model 1 (No minimum):** $18,310 total, 488 videos qualify, $0.004656 per view
• **Model 3 (CPM):** $13,693 total, 488 videos qualify, $0.003482 per view
• **Model 6 (3K minimum):** $6,685 total, 109 videos qualify, $0.001725 per view ⭐
• **Model 8 (3K + CPM bonuses):** $7,306 total, 109 videos qualify, $0.001885 per view

## Quality Filter Impact
• **Total videos:** 1,240 in dataset
• **Model 1:** Pays for 488 videos (39.4% qualification rate)
• **Model 6:** Pays for 109 videos (8.8% qualification rate)
• **Result:** 379 fewer low-performing videos paid (77.7% reduction)
• **Average views per qualified video:** ~35,500 views

## Cost Breakdown
• **Model 6 Total:** $6,685
  - Base costs: $3,430 (51% of budget)
  - Performance bonuses: $3,255 (49% of budget)
• **Model 1 Total:** $18,310
  - Base costs: $15,020 (82% of budget)
  - Performance bonuses: $3,290 (18% of budget)

## Why 3K is the Sweet Spot
• **Too low (no minimum):** Wastes budget on videos with 500-1,000 views
• **Too high (10K minimum):** Excludes quality videos with 8K-9K views
• **Just right (3K minimum):** Filters low performers, includes quality content

## ROI Metrics
• **Cost per qualified video:**
  - Model 1: $37.52 per video (but pays for 488 videos)
  - Model 6: $61.33 per video (but only pays for 109 videos)
• **Budget allocation:**
  - Model 1: 82% base / 18% bonuses (unbalanced)
  - Model 6: 51% base / 49% bonuses (balanced, rewards performance)

## Creator Impact
• **Top performers:** Unaffected - still get $30/$40 base + bonuses
• **Mid-tier (3K-10K views):** Clear incentive to reach 3K threshold
• **Low performers (<3K):** Encourages improvement or natural filtering

## Key Advantages
• Quality gate: Only pays for videos with real engagement (3K+ views)
• Budget efficiency: 63.5% cost savings vs traditional models
• Creator incentive: Still rewards top performers with bonuses
• Fair threshold: 3K views is achievable for quality content
• Platform flexibility: Works across Instagram, TikTok, YouTube
• Industry competitive: $1.73 CPM beats standard $2-$5 CPM

## Recommendation
**Implement Model 6 (3K Minimum Base)** for:
✅ Budget efficiency (63.5% savings)
✅ Creator satisfaction (fair threshold, good rates)
✅ Quality control (only pays for performing content)
✅ Scalability (lower cost enables growth)
